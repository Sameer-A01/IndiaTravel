Your Backwater Journey

Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Kerala's backwaters with our carefully crafted 3-day itinerary that combines relaxation, nature, and authentic local experiences.

Arrival at Alleppey and transfer to your traditional Kettuvallam houseboat

Welcome drink with fresh coconut water

Afternoon cruise through narrow canals and wide lagoons

Sunset viewing with traditional Kerala snacks

Overnight stay on houseboat with authentic Kerala dinner

Morning yoga session on the houseboat deck

Visit to local village to see coir-making and toddy tapping

Traditional Kerala lunch at a local family home

Afternoon canoe tour through narrow waterways

Evening Kathakali dance performance

Day 3: Nature Walk & Departure

Morning birdwatching tour in small canoe

Breakfast with local specialties like Puttu and Kadala

Guided nature walk through paddy fields

Check-out and transfer to Cochin airport
